# Card Grid Diagram Template

Generate a grid layout with multiple cards showing related concepts, categories, or items.

## Layout Structure

- **Grid**: 2x2, 2x3, 3x3, or 4x2 arrangement of cards
- **Cards**: Equal-sized rectangular cards
- **Optional Header**: Title above the grid
- **Consistent Gaps**: Equal spacing between all cards

## Visual Specifications

### Grid Container
- Background: Warm Cream (#E6E7DD) or White
- Cards float on background
- Grid aligned to center of image

### Cards
- Shape: Rounded rectangles (12-16px radius)
- Size: Uniform across all cards
- Background: White with subtle shadow OR Sage Green (#5A8669)
- Border: Optional 1px Sage Green
- Shadow: Soft, subtle (4px blur, 10% black)

### Card Content
- Icon (optional): Top of card, centered or left-aligned
- Icon style: Simple line or filled, 32-48px
- Icon color: Deep Forest (#1D442F) or Sage Green
- Title: Figtree Bold or Playfair Display, 16-22px
- Description: Figtree Regular, 12-15px, Dark Brown
- Internal padding: 20-28px

### Color Variations
- Option A: All cards same color (white), icons vary
- Option B: Cards alternate between 2 brand colors
- Option C: Each card has unique accent color from palette

### Grid Header (if present)
- Position: Above grid, full width
- Font: Playfair Display, 28-36px
- Color: Dark Brown (#403422) or Deep Forest (#1D442F)
- Alignment: Centered or left-aligned

### Background
- Solid Warm Cream (#E6E7DD)
- No patterns or decorative elements

## Typography
- Grid title: Playfair Display, 28-36px
- Card titles: Figtree Bold or Playfair Display, 16-22px
- Card descriptions: Figtree Regular, 12-15px
- Labels/tags: Figtree Medium, 10-12px

## Proportions
- Image aspect ratio: 1:1, 4:3, or 3:2
- Card gap: 16-24px between cards
- Grid padding: 48-64px from edges
- Card aspect ratio: Varies (1:1 for icon-heavy, 4:3 or 3:2 for text-heavy)

## Critical Requirements
- Perfect grid alignment
- Consistent card sizing
- All cards visually balanced
- Text hierarchy clear within each card
- Cards should feel like a cohesive set
- No logo placement
