# Detailed Checklist/Guide Template

Generate a long-form, text-heavy diagram showing a comprehensive checklist, guide, or framework with multiple sections.

## Layout Structure

- **Header Section**: Large title at top with optional subtitle
- **Body Sections**: 3-8 distinct sections, each with heading and content
- **Vertical Flow**: Content flows top to bottom
- **Section Numbering**: Optional numbered or lettered sections

## Visual Specifications

### Header
- Title: Large, Playfair Display, 32-48px
- Subtitle: Figtree, 18-22px, Dark Gray (#464644)
- Background: White or light section
- Underline or decorative element: Gold/Tan (#D5A972) thin line

### Section Headers
- Style: Bold text with left accent bar or numbered badge
- Accent bar: 4px wide, Sage Green (#5A8669) or Gold (#D5A972)
- Font: Playfair Display or Figtree Bold, 20-26px
- Color: Deep Forest (#1D442F) or Dark Brown (#403422)

### Section Content
- Background: Alternating White and very light mint (#BAD8C4 at 10%)
- Text: Figtree Regular, 14-16px, Dark Brown (#403422)
- Line height: 1.5-1.6
- Paragraphs or bullet lists

### Checklist Items
- Checkbox style: Square or circle, unfilled or with checkmark
- Checkbox color: Sage Green (#5A8669)
- Item text: Figtree, 14-16px
- Indent: Clear hierarchy for sub-items

### Callout Boxes (optional)
- Background: Gold/Tan (#D5A972) at 15% or Soft Mint (#BAD8C4)
- Border: 1px left border in accent color
- Icon: Lightbulb, star, or info icon
- Used for tips, notes, or key takeaways

### Background
- Main: White (#FFFFFF) or Off-White (#E9E9DF)
- Sections may alternate slightly for visual rhythm

## Typography
- Main title: Playfair Display Bold, 32-48px
- Section headers: Playfair Display or Figtree Bold, 20-26px
- Body text: Figtree Regular, 14-16px
- Sub-items: Figtree Regular, 12-14px
- Tips/callouts: Figtree Italic, 13-15px

## Proportions
- Image aspect ratio: 3:4 or 2:3 (tall/portrait) for long content
- Content width: 80-85% of image width, centered
- Section spacing: 32-48px between sections
- Internal padding: 24-32px within sections

## Critical Requirements
- All text must be fully legible
- Clear visual hierarchy (title > sections > content > sub-items)
- Consistent spacing throughout
- Easy to follow top-to-bottom
- Scannable section headers
- No logo placement
