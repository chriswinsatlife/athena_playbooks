# Horizontal Flow Diagram Template

Generate a horizontal process flow diagram showing sequential steps from left to right.

## Layout Structure

- **Orientation**: Strict left-to-right flow
- **Steps**: 3-6 rectangular or pill-shaped containers
- **Arrows**: Connecting arrows between each step
- **Single Row**: All elements on the same horizontal axis

## Visual Specifications

### Step Containers
- Shape: Rounded rectangles (12-16px corner radius)
- Width: Uniform, calculated to fit 3-6 steps with spacing
- Height: ~80-120px
- Background: Alternating between Sage Green (#5A8669) and Soft Mint (#BAD8C4)
- Border: None or 1px darker green

### Arrows
- Style: Simple chevron or arrow pointing right
- Color: Dark Brown (#403422) or Deep Forest (#1D442F)
- Size: Proportional to step height
- Placement: Centered between steps

### Step Numbers (optional)
- Position: Top-left corner or above each step
- Style: Circle with number inside
- Color: Gold/Tan (#D5A972) or Deep Forest (#1D442F)
- Font: Figtree, bold

### Text Layout
- Step title: Top portion, Playfair Display or Figtree Bold, 16-20px
- Step description: Below title, Figtree Regular, 12-14px
- Text color: Dark Brown (#403422) or White (on dark backgrounds)

### Background
- Solid Warm Cream (#E6E7DD)
- No decorative elements

## Typography
- Step titles: Playfair Display or Figtree Bold, 16-20px
- Descriptions: Figtree Regular, 12-14px
- Step numbers: Figtree Bold, 14-16px

## Proportions
- Image aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:1 (wide horizontal)
- Vertical centering of flow
- 48-64px padding on all sides
- 24-32px gap between steps

## Critical Requirements
- Perfectly aligned horizontal flow
- Consistent step sizing
- Clear visual progression left to right
- Arrows must clearly indicate direction
- Legible text at smaller sizes
- No logo placement
