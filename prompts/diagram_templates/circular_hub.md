# Circular Hub Diagram Template

Generate a circular/radial diagram with a central hub and surrounding elements.

## Layout Structure

- **Central Hub**: Large circle in the exact center of the image containing the main concept/title
- **Surrounding Elements**: 4-8 elements arranged in a perfect circle around the hub
- **Connectors**: Thin lines or subtle arrows connecting each outer element to the center
- **Equal Spacing**: All outer elements equidistant from center and from each other

## Visual Specifications

### Central Hub
- Circle diameter: ~20-25% of image width
- Background: Deep Forest Green (#1D442F)
- Text: White or Warm Cream, Playfair Display font, centered
- Font size: 24-32px equivalent

### Outer Elements
- Shape: Rounded rectangles or circles
- Size: ~12-15% of image width each
- Background: Sage Green (#5A8669) or Soft Mint (#BAD8C4)
- Text: Dark Brown (#403422), Figtree font
- Border: 1px darker shade or none

### Connectors
- Style: Simple straight lines or gentle curves
- Color: Sage Green (#5A8669) at 60% opacity
- Width: 2px
- Optional: Small circles at connection points

### Background
- Solid Warm Cream (#E6E7DD)
- No patterns, no gradients

## Typography
- Central hub title: Playfair Display, bold, 24-32px
- Outer element labels: Figtree, medium weight, 14-18px
- Optional sub-labels: Figtree, regular, 12-14px

## Proportions
- Image aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Outer ring radius: ~35-40% from center
- Minimum 48px padding from edges

## Critical Requirements
- Perfect circular symmetry
- Clean, minimal design
- High contrast text
- No decorative flourishes
- No logo placement
