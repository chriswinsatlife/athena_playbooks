# Comparison Columns Diagram Template

Generate a side-by-side comparison diagram with 2-4 columns showing different options, states, or approaches.

## Layout Structure

- **Columns**: 2-4 equal-width vertical columns
- **Headers**: Each column has a header/title at top
- **Content Rows**: Aligned rows of content across columns
- **Optional Dividers**: Thin vertical lines between columns

## Visual Specifications

### Column Headers
- Background: Deep Forest Green (#1D442F) or alternating brand greens
- Height: ~60-80px
- Text: White or Cream, Playfair Display, centered
- Font size: 20-28px

### Column Bodies
- Background: White or very light (#F5F5F0)
- Content: List items, icons, or descriptive text
- Internal padding: 24-32px

### Content Items
- Bullet points or checkmarks for list items
- Icons: Simple line icons in Sage Green
- Text: Figtree, 14-16px, Dark Brown (#403422)
- Spacing: 16-20px between items

### Dividers (optional)
- Color: Sage Green (#5A8669) at 30% opacity
- Width: 1px
- Style: Solid

### Highlighting
- "Preferred" or "Recommended" column can have:
  - Slightly different header color (Bright Green #559F68)
  - Subtle background tint
  - Badge or label indicating preference

### Background
- Solid Warm Cream (#E6E7DD)
- Columns float on background with subtle shadow (optional)

## Typography
- Column headers: Playfair Display, 20-28px, centered
- Item labels: Figtree Medium, 14-16px
- Item descriptions: Figtree Regular, 12-14px
- Check/X marks: Use unicode or simple icons

## Proportions
- Image aspect ratio: 4:3 or 3:2
- Equal column widths
- 32-48px gap between columns (or 1px divider)
- 48px padding from edges

## Critical Requirements
- Perfect column alignment
- Consistent row heights across columns
- Clear visual distinction between columns
- Easy to scan and compare
- No logo placement
