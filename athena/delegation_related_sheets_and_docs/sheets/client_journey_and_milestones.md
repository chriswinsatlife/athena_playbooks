## All

### 1

---

### 2

**Details:** Client
**Month 1:** Sharing context, providing feedback, making mistakes
**Month 2:** Delegating by process, not by tasks; sharing goals & thought process
**Month 3:** Expanding delegation across personal & business areas; recursive leverage
**Month 6:** Enabling EA to think/act the same way you would across the board
**Month 12:** Taking self out of the loop, pushing limits & upskilling EA
**Y2:** Delegating as a reflex, bulletproofing systems, coaching EA proactivity
**Beyond:** Main EA as liaison with key people and taking point on new projects, hiring 2nd/3rd+ EA

---

### 3

**Details:** EA
**Month 1:** Learning by immersion, nailing the basics, developing the relationship
**Month 2:** Looking for ways to add value without client as bottleneck; putting things on autopilot
**Month 3:** Being more autonomous, expanding skill set and role definition; being involved in more areas
**Month 6:** Taking things off the client's plate; getting things done without client always in-the-loop
**Month 12:** Aiming to execute some things without review / approval, shifting towards proactivity
**Y2:** Basics fully on autopilot; starting to co-own client goals and work with key people to make it happen
**Beyond:** Being the right hand and connective fabric with a broader team, onto new projects & adventures

---

### 5

**Area:** Goals
**Details:** Dynamic
**Month 1:** Taking two steps back to take 10 steps forward; learning the ropes
**Month 2:** Starting to experience leverage in basic areas; developing feedback loops & relationship
**Month 3:** EA consistently nailing email voice, scheduling, and preferences; moving beyond the basics
**Month 6:** Mundane work almost fully handed off; most time spent in zone of genius; EA owning more and more
**Month 12:** Several key business and personal workflows nearly all on autopilot; starting to experience long-term benefits like clairvoyance
**Y2:** Delegating less, but achieving more; EA dynamic more "push" than "pull"; magic starting to happen
**Beyond:** Delegating delegation itself; trending toward broader and recursive leverage

---

### 6

**Details:** Skillls
**Month 1:** Delegate both personal / business; test waters with "easy" playbooks; experiment w/ task system, voice notes, Loom, etc
**Month 2:** Move beyond the basics of inbox / calendar / travel / etc; Aggressively invest in recurring delegations + playbooks
**Month 3:** Start removing self as bottleneck; find ways to limit delegating by task; empower EA to create automations/playbooks
**Month 6:** Delegate by sharing outcomes/goals, EA providing process / ideas + working with others
**Month 12:** Empower EA to execute within limits; e.g. purchases/comms without approval, etc
**Y2:** Delegating by process and goal now lion's share; invest in enabling proactivity & guiding EA's autonomous work
**Beyond:** Leverage main EA in hub-and-spoke model with other EAs & new personnel

---

### 7

**Details:** Relationship
**Month 1:** Share tons of access / context; over-invest in giving feedback
**Month 2:** Involve EA in key communications / relationships; share nuances of preferences
**Month 3:** Inject EA into more workflows in more areas; start EA's upskilling journey
**Month 6:** Experiment with EA hiring contractors, coaches/experts, etc to pursue goals
**Month 12:** EA upskilling in other roles/areas enabling more aggressive leverage
**Y2:** Utilize EA as co-pilot for personal / professional goals; develop skill roadmap
**Beyond:** Start thinking bigger; use EA leverage on toward new ventures & goals
