## Original Notes

### 1

**Level:** 1
**Name:** Task-oriented delegation
**Leverage:** 1x - 2x
**Details:** You assign tasks as they come up and give feedback most of the time
**Criteria:** You're already here—try to exit as soon as possible
**Baseline: Average:** Most people with EAs start here, and many tragically remain here forever

---

### 2

**Details:** You frequently wonder whether you're delegating enough, or getting as much leverage as you could (should) be getting

---

### 3

**Details:** You somewhat regularly commit the cardinal sin of the delegator: doing something yourself because you can do it faster / better, or are too lazy to clarify how it should be done to someone else

---

### 5

**Level:** 2
**Name:** Process-oriented delegation
**Leverage:** 3x - 5x
**Details:** You see yourself as an algorithm (e.g. what rules do I use when I'm booking flights?), and can consistently translate this into steps for your EA or other outsourced teammates
**Criteria:** You have at least 5 active playbooks
**Baseline: Average:** Average people reach this level after 3-6 months
**Baseline: Athenians:** Athenians typically reach this level by the end of month 1
**Baseline: Top 10%:** Athena's Top 10% reach this level within two weeks

---

### 6

**Details:** You always review a task you're about to delegate to see whether it can be transformed into a broader SOP or system
**Criteria:** You've delegated at least two things in 4/8 personal areas

---

### 7

**Details:** You regularly give feedback to your EA on refining SOPs/systems as edge cases are discovered, new situations arise, or priorities change
**Criteria:** You send voice notes 5x/wk or more, unless you truly can't do voice as a platform

---

### 8

**Details:** You are likely delegating and providing feedback so much that you are using voice on a daily basis

---

### 10

**Level:** 3
**Name:** Goal-oriented delegation
**Leverage:** 5x - 10x
**Details:** You and your EA have a clear picture of your personal and business goals, near-term & long-term
**Criteria:** Your EA has suggested 3 tasks related to your goals which you accepted in last 28d
**Baseline: Average:** Average people typically never reach this level; those who do tend to take 3-5 years
**Baseline: Athenians:** Athenians generally reach this level within 6-18 months
**Baseline: Top 10%:** Athena's top 10% can reach this level within 3m-6m

---

### 11

**Details:** You regularly assess your life and work to gauge whether you are on track to reach your goals, if your velocity can be improved, and how your EA can help
**Criteria:** You have at least 10 active playbooks, which your EA has iterated without your input

---

### 12

**Details:** Your EA is so intimately knowledgable with your goals and thought processes that they can consistently suggest tasks that you approve (which you encourage)
**Criteria:** Your EA has suggested a playbook related to a goal, which you now actively use

---

### 13

**Details:** Your EA is in direct contact with all other individuals who are related to your goals, and they often act as liaison for hiring experts, coaches, and contractors; moreover, your significant other + coworkers route goal-related tasks to the EA if req'd
**Criteria:** You've delegated something in all 8 personal areas

---

### 14

**Details:** You give so much feedback and have developed such a strong relationship with your EA that they are confident enough to act autonomously and make small mistakes

---

### 16

**Level:** 4
**Name:** Clairvoyant delegation
**Leverage:** 10x / beyond
**Details:** You have an abundant sense of time affluence and self-rate 10/10 on productivity
**Criteria:** Your EA has done 10 tasks in the last quarter which you have not suggested nor approved, and at least one of these tasks was new
**Baseline: Average:** Typical delegators not only fail to reach this level, but they may not believe it exists
**Baseline: Athenians:** Only the top 10% of Athenians reach this level (although we're working on making it easier)
**Baseline: Top 10%:** Those who reach this level virtually always take more than 18-24 months, often 3-10 years

---

### 17

**Details:** Your and your EA's actions are in total alignment with your greater purpose in work and life, with almost zero friction
**Criteria:** Most of your personal and work tasks are run through playbooks; fewer than 20% of EA tasks are outside the scope of a playbook

---

### 18

**Details:** You worry about almost nothing other than the most complex issues you are unique among the world's humans in being able to solve
**Criteria:** Your EA has written and proposed a complete playbook without your input, which they did not source from Athena's internal playbook library

---

### 19

**Details:** You trust your EA like a family member and they act almost as if a second brain; they regularly discover and execute tasks without your input; the first you hear of many tasks is a note confirming that it's done
