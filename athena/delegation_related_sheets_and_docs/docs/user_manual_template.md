  1. Question \#1: *Do you prefer to work with people who make decisions quickly with a margin for error or people who to take more time with decisions but have a higher degree of confidence?*

  2. Question \#2: *Do you prefer to work with people who are motivated by achieving small milestones or pursuing big-picture outcomes?*

  3. Question \#3: *Do you prefer to work with people who collaborate best synchronously or asynchronously when making a big decision?*  
     1. Live collaboration vs. independent processing  
  4. Question \#4: *When working towards a goal, do you prefer to work with people whose strength is having defined processes or just the end goal in mind?*  
     1. Working with detailed instructions or working with an end goal in mind  
  5. Question \#5: *Do you prefer to work with people who thrive on autonomy, or people who work best with schedules and structure?*  
     1. Flexibility vs. structure in your work environment  
  6. Question \#6: *In decision making, do you prefer to work with people who rely heavily on outside information and data, or people who are more driven by instinct?*  
     1. Relying on data vs. intuition in decision making  
  7. Question \#7: *Do you prefer to work with people who bring emotion and passion to work, or people who maintain a psychological distance between work and life?*  
     1. Emotional investment vs. emotional separation in your work
