   # [**Discovery Call Script**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ArVKRxRMicPrHKt9LwHRnBjPJTcbwL7hYguWPghxwcs/edit) **(Old)**

1. Where is your home base? Is that also your primary work location?   
2. Any other places that you spend your time in?  
3. What are the hats that you wear in your personal life? Who is \<Client\> in his/her personal life?   
4. What about in your professional life, what are the different hats that you wear?   
5. Knowing that you wear all of these different hats professionally and personally, who would you say are your key stakeholders or the important people around you that your assistant should take note of?   
6. If there's a typical day in your life, what does your Monday to Friday look like? What time do you usually wake up, start your work, end your work, and also wrap up for the day?   
7. What about on weekends? Does any of the work bleed into your weekends?   
8. What's your idea of a perfect day or a perfect week? What does it look like for you?  
9. Where do you think is your time best spent right now in your professional life?   
10. Where do you think you're not making the best use of your time in your professional life?   
11. What about in your personal life? Where do you think is your time best spent right now in your personal life?  
12. Where do you think you're not making the best use of your time right now in your personal life?  
13. What are your professional goals for the next three to six months?   
14. What about your personal goals? What are your personal goals for the next three to six months?  
15. What are the things that energizes you or you're passionate about in general?  
16. What about the things that drain your energy in general? What are those?  
17.  Can you share more or some honest or unfiltered things about you as an individual that would be beneficial for your assistant to know in preparation to working with you?   
18. What are some work habits that you want to share about you, like anything that you were specific with or very particular with about the way you work? Any of that?  
19. How does one earn trust with you? What's your trust building or trust earning framework?   
20. What are your communication channels that you often use?   
21. For email, are you using Gmail? Outlook? Superhuman?  
22. So have we given thought on what hours or time do you want him or her to work? What are your preferred schedules? 

    # [**Discovery Call Script**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ArVKRxRMicPrHKt9LwHRnBjPJTcbwL7hYguWPghxwcs/edit) **(New)**

1. Where is the main home base for you?  
2. What travels do you have scheduled this year?  
3. Talk to me about some of the professional hats that you wear.  
4. What personal relationships do you hold dear and that your assistant should know about?  
5. Who would you consider the key stakeholders in your life, both personally and professionally?  
6. Can you walk me through what your current schedule is like on the weekdays from the time that you get up to the time that you go to bed?  
7. On the weekends, is it reserved more for personal time or do you find yourself doing a lot of work still on the weekends?  
8. Do you already have an idea of what shift schedule you'd like your assistant to work in?  
9. What time does your EA work, so I can figure out the schedule for the Athena assistant?  
10. Walk me through what your ideal schedule looks like. If there's anything that in your schedule that you'd like to change, or if there's anything that you'd like to see on your calendar consistently, what would that be?  
11. Where is your time best spent professionally? What are the tasks that you're supposed to be focused on and honed in on?  
12. How can the Athena assistant support you in being able to free up time to do what you're supposed to do?  
13. What are some of the tasks that you see yourself delegating to the assistant?  
14. How about help with inbox management or calendar management? Can you see your Athena assistant helping you out with those tasks as well?  
15. What tasks are you supposed to be doing in your personal life that your assistant can help with or make sure that you have space for?  
16. Do you have any active goals that you would want to hit in the next three or six months personally?  
17. What are some of the professional goals or milestones that you would want to hit in the next three to six months?  
18. What are some of the things that energize you?  
19. What are some of the activities that drain you?  
20. What would you do with more time once you have the support that you need?  
21. Talk to me about your experience with an assistant. What worked and what do you like seeing?  
22. Talk to me about a time where your relationship with your assistant wasn't that great. What areas did you see that you don't want to work with them again?  
23. Do you already have onboarding materials prepared for the second Athena assistant, or is this something that you would still have to work on with the current/first assistant?  
24. Talk to me about some honest, unfiltered things about yourself that your assistant should know for you to be able to have a harmonious relationship.  
25. What are some of your work habits? For example, if you have meetings, how long would you like the meetings to go? Do you need a break before and after the meetings?  
26. What are the things that your assistant needs to do in order to earn or gain your trust?  
27. What's the one thing that you are currently working on improving as a leader?  
28. Do you prefer to work with people who make decisions quickly with a margin of error, or people who take more time with decisions but have a higher degree of confidence?  
29. Do you prefer people who are motivated by achieving small milestones or pursuing big-picture outcomes?  
30. Do you prefer to work with people who collaborate best synchronously or asynchronously?  
31. When working towards a goal, do you prefer to work with people whose strength is having defined processes or just the end goal in mind?  
32. Do you prefer to work with people who thrive on autonomy or people who work best with schedules and structure?  
33. In decision-making, do you prefer to work with people who rely heavily on data or people who are more intuitive in their decision-making?  
34. Do you prefer to work with people who bring emotion and passion to work or people who maintain a psychological distance between work and life?  
35. What communication channels do you use and what are your preferences around them?

# [**4 Week Check In Script**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1U8epSAXbDdg6vhvell1H8cAHoIYRM2rTWqlLpsxqZew/edit#heading=h.b89t3j6mjhlp)

1. Have you been able to keep up with the syllabus, specifically completing Inbox 1-3 and Calendar 1-3?  
2. What about the partnership rituals we discussed? How have they integrated into your daily routine, and what systems have you started to develop for managing recurring tasks?  
3. When we had a kickoff call, I shared that the key to a successful partnership is a lot of Access and Communication. How would you rate yourself in these two areas as a delegator?  
4. Is there anything specific that I can do to assist your XPs in meeting or exceeding these expectations?  
5. Are there areas where they might benefit from additional guidance or coaching?  
6. If your score is below a 7, could you share what adjustments or improvements would elevate your experience to a 10? Your feedback is crucial for us to achieve and maintain a satisfaction level of 7 or higher.

   # [**8 Week Check In Script**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1uTEQPcFPjHtr-EMrlOtnujvzHiHbtcApobKREvbkPDs/edit)

1. I'm interested in learning about the area where you've received the most significant support from XP. Could you share which aspect of her assistance has been most beneficial, and why do you believe it's been successful?"  
2. \<Client\>, could you identify an area where you'd like to see your partnership with XP evolve? What steps do you envision for yourself and the XP to achieve this?"  
3. Lastly, I'd like to touch on the importance of expressing gratitude. Both of you have been putting in a tremendous effort, and it's crucial to recognize each other's contributions. \<Client\>, what would you like your XP to know?"

   # [**12 Week Check In Script**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jDOHfXHKhl6TGknRVCH9yuUpI6H8ZFuY7g5M8sA5lRU/edit#heading=h.9bqknjrl4yvf)

6. Looking back at your priorities and goals when we first started. Have we been able to add value to your life and are there areas you wish we could do more for you?  
7. Could you share what adjustments or improvements would elevate your experience to a 10? Your feedback is crucial for us to achieve and maintain a satisfaction level of 7 or higher.
