## Sales-Led vs. Product-Led Growth Mix üìä

- **Mix and Evolution:** In practice, Oceans uses a hybrid approach: *sales-led acquisition, product-led retention/expansion*. In summary, Oceans‚Äô growth model is **sales-assisted and service-led**.
